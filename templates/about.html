About Us

Our story, mission, and values.

Who We Are

Beatwell Upholstery is a premier upholstery service provider based in Kariba, Zimbabwe. We specialize in bringing comfort and style to your home, boat, vehicle, and outdoor spaces.

With years of experience and a deep understanding of Kariba’s unique climate challenges—including intense heat and sun exposure—we carefully select materials that ensure durability without compromising on aesthetics.

Mission

To provide high-quality, durable, and stylish upholstery solutions that enhance the comfort and longevity of our customers' furniture and equipment.

Vision

To be the leading upholstery service provider in the region, known for our craftsmanship, reliability, and customer satisfaction.
